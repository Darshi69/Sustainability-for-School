# Sustainable @ School

*Small Changes, Big Impact!*  
This project explains how we can make our school more eco-friendly, reduce waste, save energy, and protect nature.

---

##  What is Sustainability?

Sustainability means *using resources wisely so that future generations can also enjoy them.*  
It focuses on:
- Saving water
- Saving energy 
- Reducing waste
- Protecting the environment 

---

### Why Should Schools Be Sustainable?

A sustainable school:
- Reduces pollution and waste 
- Saves money on electricity and water 
- Keeps the school clean and healthy 
- Teaches students green habits 

---

##  Ideas to Make Our School Sustainable

###  Reduce, Reuse & Recycle
- Use separate waste bins
- Avoid plastic bottles and bags
- Reuse paper and notebooks

###  Save Water
- Turn off taps after use
- Fix leaking taps quickly
- Use water wisely in labs & washrooms

###  Save Energy
- Switch off fans/lights when not needed
- Use natural daylight in classrooms
- Encourage solar panels (if possible)

---

##  School Green Projects

Students can organize:
- Tree planting drives 
- Composting food waste 
- Paper recycling workshop 
- Eco-club activities
